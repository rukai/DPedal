Assembling

  1. Ensure you have everything in the BOM
  2. Solder hotswap sockets to PCB
  3. Solder pico to PCB
  4. Place PCB in pico base
  5. Place switch plate on top of pico base
  6. Screw the switch plate on to the pico base
  7. Clip the switches into their sockets
  8. Put the dpad between the two base pieces and push the pieces together.
  9. Screw the right base to the switch plate
  10. Push the 2 buttons into the 2 empty switches
